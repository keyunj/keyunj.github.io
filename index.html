About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Jie Zhou and Asst. Prof. Jiangjian Feng since 2017.
My Bachelor's degree is received from the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University.
My research interests lie in computer vision and machine learning, particularly medical image analysis, fingerprint recognition, and human-computer interaction techniques.
